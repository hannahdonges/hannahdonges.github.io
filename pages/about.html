Hi there, I'm Hannah!

I am currently in my final year of studying a Bachelor of Engineering (Mechatronic Systems) at ANU, and work part-time in engineering support for a computer vision company. When I’m not busy on a new tech project or cramming for exams, in my free time (albeit limited) I love creating art in any and all possible forms.

Here on my site you will find an eclectic assortment of past works including – but not limited to – painting, embroidery, metal casting and woodwork. I enjoy working with a large range of mediums as each requires a unique set of skills, techniques, and tools – presenting new challenges and allowing me to broaden my understanding of creating.

Much of the inspiration for my works is drawn from the beauty of the world around me, including both natural forms and iconic man-made structures. Through my work, I wish to capture and express this in a way which may invoke a sense of wonder and inspire others to look more purposefully at world, so that they too may be inspired. I also enjoy working with movement and functionality within my designs to create items of use.

My website is aimed at anyone interested in hand-crafted art, as well as those looking for inspiration to pick up a new hobby. If you like what you see and want to get in touch, contact me here!
